About Alzheimer's

Currently Alzheimer's has no cure, but treatments for symptoms are available and research to find a cure continues. Alzheimer's treatments cannot stop Alzheimer's from progressing, but they can temporarily slow down the symptoms. Today, there is a worldwide effort under way to find ways to treat the disease.
